Watson Lab Behavioral Box : BB-## not backing up to Overseer

Created by Anjesh Ghimire on Oct 14, 2020

Problem

One or multiple BB computers is not properly backing up videos/event data/ both to Overseer. 

Solution

1. Check to see if F: drive on Overseer is full. If F: is full, BB's cannot backup to Overseer. 

  • Tunnel into Overseer.
  • Open "File Explorer." Click on "This PC."
  • If F: is full, it will have to be replaced. 

  • (TO BE FINISHED) Make sure the same folders are on the new F: drive.
  • Launch "Backup and Sync"...
  • Place old hard drive in ESD proof plastic bag. Label Properly and store in.....
