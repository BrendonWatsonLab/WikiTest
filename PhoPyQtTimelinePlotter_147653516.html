Watson Lab Behavioral Box : PhoPyQtTimelinePlotter

Created by Pho Hale, last modified on Aug 04, 2020

Link: https://github.com/CommanderPho/PhoPyQtTimelinePlotter

Platform: Cross-platform

Prerequisites: Anaconda

Displays an interactive timeline depicting events alongside video files.

Video files can be played back in real-time or at variable speeds for the purpose of reviewing events and scoring behaviors.
