Watson Lab Behavioral Box : 12V PSU Supply Wire Assembly

Created by Pho Hale, last modified on Oct 15, 2019

The wire that runs from the 12V DC Power Supply to the Power Distribution Board Assembly to provide power to its rails.

It's made of two ~24" wires, one red, and one black, lightly twisted.


One end has a Barrel Jack Breakout - Female and the other has stripped wires.
