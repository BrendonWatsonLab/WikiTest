Watson Lab Behavioral Box : Regular or Sucrose water dispensers not dispensing

Created by Anjesh Ghimire, last modified on Oct 14, 2020

Problem

Finger pokes (10+) don't lead to regular or sucrose water dispensing.  

Solution

1) Try First (quick):

  • Insert an appropriate sized plunger into the water syringe. Finger poke to see if water dispenses properly. 

Often times, the addition of this slight pressure from the syringe will be enough to get proper dispensing. 


2) If water dispenser still does not work:

  • Hold solenoid for the improperly dispensing water. Finger poke.

If the "click/vibration" of the solenoid is felt, the solenoid works properly and is not the source of the problem. 

If nothing is felt, solenoid may be cause of dispensing problem.
