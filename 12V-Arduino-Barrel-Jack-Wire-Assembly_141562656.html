Watson Lab Behavioral Box : 12V Arduino Barrel Jack Wire Assembly

Created by Pho Hale, last modified by Anjesh Ghimire on May 21, 2021

A ~21" long cable with a male barrel jack connector on one end and 2 wires on the other.

Labeled with "Arduino 12V" on manila tape.


From I.Cerda Thesis: 

This assembly consists of a 21”-long cable connected to the Power Distribution Board on one end and a male barrel jack connector on the other end. It provides 12 V DC power to an Arduino Mega on the Arduino-Motor Shield Construct.
