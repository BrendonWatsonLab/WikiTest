Watson Lab Behavioral Box : Circuit Panel Assembly

Created by Pho Hale, last modified by Anjesh Ghimire on May 21, 2021

From I.Cerda Thesis: 

The Circuit Panel Assembly shown takes in 12 V of power from the AC/DC converter and
distributes it to key components of the Digital Homecage, including the Light Relay Station, the Arduino, and
the Motor Shield. The Circuit Panel Assembly consists of a 12 V Arduino Barrel Jack Wire Assembly, a Buck
Converter Power Assembly, a 12 V Visible Light Relay Power Wire Assembly, and a Power Distribution Board
Assembly. It also houses the Water Solenoid Valve Driving Circuit.

  • 12V Arduino Barrel Jack Wire Assembly
  • 12V Buck Converter Power Wire Assembly
  • 12V Visible Light Relay Power Wire Assembly
  • Buck Converter to 9V Motorshield Power Wire Assembly
  • Power Distribution Board Assembly
    • 12V PSU Supply Wire Assembly

White Barrel Jack 2-Conductor Cable Assembly

  1. Create a Buck Converter to 9V Motorshield Power Wire Assembly by cutting 28" from the non-connector end of the White Barrel Jack 2-Conductor Cable Assembly. Approximately 45" remain on the barrel jack side of White Barrel Jack 2-Conductor Cable Assembly.
  2. Create an 12V Arduino Barrel Jack Wire Assembly by cutting ~21" from the barrel jack end of the White Barrel Jack 2-Conductor Cable Assembly. You are then left with an ~24" 2-conductor cable.
  3. Create a 12V Buck Converter Power Wire Assembly by cutting ~4" from the remaining 2-conductor cable. You should be left with ~20".
  4. The remaining ~20" of cable is the 12V Visible Light Relay Power Wire Assembly.
