Watson Lab Behavioral Box : Exterior Encasement

Created by Pho Hale, last modified by Abheek Mukherjee on Nov 26, 2019

Name: Exterior Encasement

Shortname: Exterior Encasement

Ordering Link: See Ann Arbor Plastics quote

#6 x 3/4" Screws : 2 per box

#8 x 2" Drywall Screws : 18 per box

Description:

The white outermost box that houses everything else. Constructed from white ¾ Foam PVC ordered from Ann Arbor Plastics. 

Notes:


  1. Make sure all sides of boxes are in the proper orientation as shown in pictures above.
  2. Attach the box side with large hole to the bottom of the box on the short left side with drill and screws. You will need to drill three holes on the bottom of the large hole box side and make sure to mark the holes to the mid-line of the bottom box side.  2 screws will go in the corners and one screw will be on the midpoint in the bottom of the side.
  3. Attach the box side with the small hole to the right long side of the box. You will need to drill 2 screws of the side of the box that you just attached. Make sure holes are marked, in the mid-line of the piece being attached, and also aligned with each other. One screw will be placed at the top of the side and one on the bottom, just above the bottom corner screw placed on the last step.  Check alignment of sides at all steps. 
  4. Attach the long box side with no holes to the left long side.  Make sure holes are marked, in the mid-line of the piece being attached, and also aligned with each other. One screw will be placed at the top of the side and one on the bottom, just above the bottom corner screw placed on step 2. Check alignment of sides at all steps. 
  5. Attach the remaining piece on the short right side. You will need to drill three holes on the bottom and 2 holes on the side. Make sure all holes are aligned with the mid-line and all sides of the box are aligned with each other.  Screws will be placed on the bottom corner, midpoint, other bottom corner, both top corners, and two screws will be placed above each of the bottom corner screws. 
  6. Flip the box over to bottom side  and drill screws in the 4 corners and midpoints of the bottom of the box. Make sure the holes are aligned to the mid-line and the bottom side is aligned.
