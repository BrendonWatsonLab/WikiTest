Watson Lab Behavioral Box : Water System Assembly

Created by Pho Hale, last modified on Oct 15, 2019

When building a new water system for a behavioral box.


Water Subsystem



Cut pieces of Tygon Water Tubing to lengths ~12”, ~26”, and ~1”.

This is a total of ~40" (1.016 [m]) of tubing per half-system, and there are two half-systems per box. 


Step-by-step guide

PictureStep

  • Attach a clear Leur-Lock Connectors Valve to the end of the 12” long tubing.
    • Attach the other end to the middle water port (#2) of the Solenoid.
  • Attach the Blunted Needle to the clear Leur-Lock Connectors.

  • Attach an opaque white Leur-Lock Connectors Valve to the end of the 26” long tubing.
    • Attach the other end to the water port farthest from the Solenoid pins (#1).
  • Attach a Water Syringe to the opaque white Leur-Lock Connectors.

  • Fill about ¼ of the 1” long tubing with Epoxy from one of its ends. Wait for it to dry.
    • After it dries, attach the unfilled end to the Solenoid water port nearest the pins (#3).
