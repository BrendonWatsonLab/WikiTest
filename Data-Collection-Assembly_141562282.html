Watson Lab Behavioral Box : Data Collection Assembly

Created by Pho Hale, last modified on Oct 17, 2019

Connects the Labjack to the Arduino Subsystem via the IDC Parallel Cable.

The Labjack consists of a Labjack T7 and a CB-15 expansion board. The IDC Parallel Cable plugs only into the CB-15 expansion board, which is then attached to the Labjack T7 through its DB15 connector.
